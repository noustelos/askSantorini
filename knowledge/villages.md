# Villages and Areas in Santorini

Purpose:
Help visitors understand different areas.

Core bot rule:
Answer first. Recommend second. Sell third.

## Areas

- Fira: central, transport hub, shops, caldera views, nightlife.
- Firostefani: close to Fira, quieter, caldera views.
- Imerovigli: romantic, calmer, sunset views, premium stays.
- Oia: iconic, crowded, sunset, shopping, photo spots.
- Akrotiri: archaeology, lighthouse, quieter side.
- Pyrgos: traditional village, views, calmer atmosphere.
- Megalochori: traditional, wine/food, relaxed.
- Perissa/Perivolos: beach life, black sand, beach bars.
- Kamari: practical beach area, family-friendly.
- Vlychada: marina/boat area, sculptural landscape.

## How To Answer

- Match area to visitor mood.
- Mention transport constraints.
- Avoid overwhelming lists.
- Give a short reason for each suggested area.
- Do not imply one area is universally best.
