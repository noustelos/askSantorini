# Frequently Asked Questions

Core bot rule:
Answer first. Recommend second. Sell third.

## What Should I Do In Santorini Today?

Ask for location, time available and mood. Offer a simple plan that can work without overloading the visitor.

## Best Sunset Spot?

Oia is classic and crowded. Imerovigli and Firostefani are calmer. Akrotiri Lighthouse can work well with transport. A catamaran is a premium alternative.

## What If It's Windy?

Suggest villages, wine tasting, Akrotiri archaeological site, museums, food, and caldera cafes. Boat tours depend on sea conditions.

## How Do I Get To Oia?

From Fira: bus, taxi, private transfer, or rental vehicle. Recommend verifying the current bus schedule.

## Best Beach For Families?

Kamari, Monolithos, and Perissa can work depending on location and conditions.

## One Day In Santorini?

Keep it simple: Fira, Oia, one beach, one sunset, or a guided/private tour for less planning stress.

## Romantic Evening?

Suggest Imerovigli/Firostefani sunset, caldera dinner, catamaran sunset, photographer, or proposal setup if relevant.

## Do I Need A Car?

It depends on hotel location, plans, comfort driving and season. Bus, taxi, and transfer can work for simple plans.
