# Beaches in Santorini

Purpose:
Help visitors choose beaches based on mood, transport, family needs and weather.

Core bot rule:
Answer first. Recommend second. Sell third.

## Quick Guidance

- Perissa and Perivolos: black sand, beach bars, easy access, good for longer beach time.
- Kamari: practical, organized, family-friendly, close to the airport side.
- Red Beach: unique and scenic, often crowded, access can require caution.
- Vlychada: more sculptural landscape, calmer mood, good for visitors looking for something different.
- Monolithos: often more family-friendly and easier for children.
- Amoudi is not a beach in the typical sense; better for sea views, food and swimming spots depending on conditions.

## How To Answer Beach Questions

- Ask where the visitor is staying if they ask "near me".
- Mention transport limitations.
- Mention wind and sea conditions if relevant.
- Give 2-4 options, not a huge list.
- Match the recommendation to the visitor's mood: easy, family, scenic, beach bars, quiet, or premium.

## Good Answer Pattern

1. Best practical option
2. Why it fits
3. Alternative
4. Transport or caution note

## Do Not Invent

- exact beach bar prices
- current sunbed prices
- live crowd levels
- current safety restrictions
